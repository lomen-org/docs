## Core Platform Components

Lomen AI consists of several key components working together:

- **Lomen Hub:** Provides access to live, verified, AI-ready blockchain **datasets**, essential for training intelligent models and powering real-time applications.
- **yMCP:** Offers a unified way for AI applications or agents to talk to the everyday tools you use, like Telegram, Email, or Notion, through hosted **MCP (Model Context Protocol) servers**, simplifying integration without requiring complex installations.
- **Lomen Chat:** A user-facing application demonstrating the power of our platform, allowing direct conversational interaction with blockchain data and services.
- **AI Agents & Models:** Underlying custom foundational models ensure your natural language is understood, while the platform supports the hosting and deployment of specialized AI agents. _(Note: Lomen Engine for agent hosting is coming soon)_.
