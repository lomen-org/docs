**What is Lomen?**
Lomen helps you invest, rebalance, and manage strategies directly from your wallet. You stay in control at all times.

**What are "baskets"?**
Baskets are curated sets of assets or strategies packaged together. Each basket is transparent. You can see exactly what's inside, how it works, and how it earns.

**Is my money safe?**
Yes. Lomen is fully self-custodial. Your assets stay in your wallet, and you maintain control. You can enter or exit any time.

**Do I need technical knowledge to use Lomen?**
Not at all. Lomen is built to be simple and intuitive. Even complex financial actions are made accessible and easy to understand.

**How do I start?**
Just visit app.getlomen.com, sign in with your wallet, and pick a basket that suits your goals. Everything takes a few clicks.

**Can I change my basket later?**
Absolutely. You can modify, switch, or rebalance your basket anytime with one click.

**Do I need to manually track my returns?**
No. Your dashboard gives you clear insights into what you earn, what's inside your basket, and how your assets are performing.

**What networks does Lomen support?**
Lomen supports Solana.
